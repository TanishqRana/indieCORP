People Aren't Hearing All the Music

Beats by Dr. Dre (Beats) is a leading audio brand founded in 2006 by Dr. Dre and Jimmy Iovine. Through its family of premium consumer headphones, earphones, and speakers, Beats has introduced an entirely new generation to the possibilities of premium sound entertainment. The brand’s continued success helps bring the energy, emotion, and excitement of playback in the recording studio back to the listening experience for music lovers worldwide. Beats was acquired by Apple Inc. in July 2014.

Testimonials

Unleash Your Audio Experience!!!!

I see a lot of reviews stating these headphones are not that good. I was getting a little scared that I wasted my money. Until I received them. These are the best sounding headphones I ever had. I know everybody is different and yes, they focus more on bass than anything else, the mids are okay but the highs are great, but these work for me, well worth the money. The control talk feature is cool too. I read people couldn't hear you or hear background noise but I talked to a half dozen different people and they said I sounded great.

Mickey Jones

These are my first premium pair of headphones, but I'm extremely satisfied with my Beats Solo in the month that I've had them. The sound quality is superb, and I've often stayed awake late at night much longer than intended because I loved hearing my music through the Beats and couldn't put them down. I love hearing and feeling lots of bass in my music, and this pair of headphones is perfect for exactly that. They sound great for a variety of different music from rock to hip-hop to electronic/dubstep. Also they are light enough and portable enough to wear for going on a run, although the cushions around the ear may get a little more dirty that way, and the tangle-free cord is great for running as well.

Andrew

I got these Beats a little over 2 years ago back when they were really popular. To this day, they still work incredibly well. I only had one cord not work anymore (since it comes with two), but it's mainly my fault for not taking proper care of it. Although these headphones are great, I'm not too impressed with the quality based on the music I listen to. These headphones are really only good for hip hop & rap. I listen to a mix of that, but also a lot of alternative and rock music, and I find myself having to raise the volume uncomfortably high to be able to hear it clearly.

Becky Myers
